Trading strategy: RSI

Development of automatic relative strenght index trading strategy.

Basic theory

The Relative Strenght Index is a simple oscillator used in technical analysis to measure how much the price of an asset is overvaluated or undervaluated. The index move on a scale from zero to 100 and traditionally a reading of 70 or above indicates an overbought situation. Meanwhile, a reading of 30 or below indicates an oversold condition.

The RSI is calculated with two step. First, it's calculated the average RSI for a look-back period, usually 14 days. With the equation: $$ RSI_{one} = 100 - \left[ \frac{100}{1 + \frac{Average gain}{Avergare loss}} \right] $$ After that calculation is done for the look-back period, the data is used to calculate the RSI for the day. $$ RSI_{two} = 100 - \left[ \frac{100}{1 + \frac{(Previous Average Gain\times 13) + Current Gain}{(Previous Average Loss\times 13) + Current Loss}} \right] $$

Development

The development of the system began getting the data from Binance using Websockets to get a continuous stream of data. While the information arrive to the program, it is pre-processed to a format easy to use and the system implement the strategy. If there is a chance, the program set an OCO order on the platform and finish the cycle.

On practice, the system was deployed on the test chain of Binance in the BTCUSDT pair. The strategy worked on the 15 minutes stream with standard look-back period of 14. The top and bottom level were set to 75 and 25, respectively. Finally, the OCO orders were adjusted, to reduce risk, with stop Loss 1% of losses and take profits on 5% of gains.

Results

After a week of testing with bearish feeling on the cryptocurrency market. The system report gains of 10.16%, this may result in the fact that BTCUSDT is lateralizing between 17,000 and 24,000, the situation offer a great opportunity to this kind of strategy.

The strategy worked very well. Yet, it is necessary more testing time to have a conclusive idea of the effectiveness of the strategy before be deployed on a real account. Meanwhile, it will be explored other strategies to be integrated.
