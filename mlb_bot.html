Mercado Libre bot on Telegram

Custom Telegram bot to receive alerts of the products on Mercado Libre Brasil

The Project

The objective was the development of a tool that would be able to extend the Mercado Libre Brasil API. The client will receive, in real time and directly to their mobile phone, notifications about products of interest that are published.

The bot

The program was built, solving 2 tasks separately. First, a module or script in charge of obtaining the new products that are published, and on the other hand, one in charge of sending this information to the user. At the beginning, the objective was to receive notifications through Whatsapp, however, it was decided, together with the client, to use the Telegram platform.

The section of the program in charge of the API, since by construction it does not offer the possibility of only obtaining new products, the script was designed to keep track of all the products of interest in a database that will serve as a point of comparison for new products. In this way, every 10 minutes the program calls the API for the required product, then it is compared with the data and if it passes the test, it is sent to the user through the second part.

The module in charge of Telegram consists on a bot of this platform, it is private by client requirement. The bot works as a front-end for data acquisition from users and as a base to send the information obtained in the previous section. This bot was designed with the ability to offer help to the user at any time, manages the database with the records and products to search for, handles the addition of filters or search requirements entered by the user (they are passed to the previous section if they exist) and fulfills, with its main function, to notify the user about new publications.

Currently, the bot is functional on the client's server, with a copy under development to optimize visual aspects and add more features.
